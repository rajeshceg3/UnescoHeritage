Design North Star

“Would anyone ask if this experience is artful, tasteful, and richly detailed—and hear an immediate yes?”

  • Atmosphere first: every screen feels curated, cinematic, and intentional.
  • Context-rich storytelling: show meaning, not just names and dates.
  • Accessible delight: keyboard support, live feedback, and reduced-motion respect.
  • Reflective interaction: favorites persist, progress is visible, and state feels alive.
