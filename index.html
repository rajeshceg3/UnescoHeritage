Design North Star

“Is this the absolute delightful and joyful experience possible for a user?”

  • Effortless: instant feedback, clear controls, zero confusion.
  • Inclusive: keyboard-friendly, accessible, and readable for everyone.
  • Emotionally rich: beauty, wonder, and a sense of discovery.
  • Reliable: graceful loading, resilient interactions, and safe fallbacks.
  • Continuously improving: save favorites and learn what users love.
